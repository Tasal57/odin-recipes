Rice Recipe description

This rice recipe is popular all over the world. It was oroginated in the early 1800's and is still indulged by many to this day. A dish that deserves nothing but admmiration, and of course all praises to the most high for blessing us with it.

Ingredients list

  • salt
  • olive oil
  • pepper
  • spicy rice powder
  • water

Steps

  1. wash the rice
  2. add rice to boiling water in the pot
  3. add salt, pepper, and rice powder to pot
  4. cook the rice for 20 minutes
  5. turn off the heat and let it sit for 5 minutes
