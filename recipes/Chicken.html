Chicken Recipe Description

This Chicken has elegent flavour which sticks onto the tongue for hours. The recipe was discovered in late 1900's in turkiye and has quickly found it's way in numerous conutries.

Ingredients list

  • salt
  • flour
  • pepper
  • olive oil

steps

  1. Wash the chicken
  2. add salt and pepper to the chicken
  3. roll the chicken in flour
  4. fry the chicken in oil until crispy
